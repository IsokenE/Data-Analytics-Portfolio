Projects

1. Sales Report Dashboard (Excel)

Built an Executive Summary Sales Report analysing over 7,900 transactions and ₦1.35M in total revenue. Broke down revenue by region, product, customer, and quarter across 2010–2011 to identify top-performing products and regions.

Tools:
Excel | Pivot Tables | Data Cleaning


2. HR Report Tracker (Power BI)

Built an interactive HR dashboard covering 1,000 employees and ₦74M in total salary. Visualised headcount by department, gender, and city, average salary by department, and headcount trends over time.

Tools:
Power BI | DAX | Data Modelling


3. Financial Report Analysis (Power BI)

Analysed financial performance data totalling ₦118.73M in sales and ₦16.89M in profit. Broke down profit margin, profit by product and segment, and sales by country across 2013–2014.

Tools:
Power BI | DAX | Power Query


4. Healthcare Analytics Dashboard (Group Project)

Collaborated with a team to build a healthcare performance report covering 54,966 patients and $1.40bn in total billing. Analysed patient demographics, admission trends (2019–2024), medical conditions, insurance providers, and length of stay to support insights for health policy decisions.

Tools:
Power BI | DAX | Power Query | Teamwork
